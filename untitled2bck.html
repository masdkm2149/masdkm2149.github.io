BullseyeTracker is a web-based scoring application that simplifies all the challenges involved in Bullseye Pistol Leagues. Following the brand’s philosophy, I created a minimal logomark that would reflect company goals. The mark, a flattened illustration of a bullseye pistol outlined by borders, conveys activity, simplicity, and performance.
